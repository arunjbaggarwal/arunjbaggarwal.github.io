A Few Accomplishments

Here are a few of my achievements from University and in employment.

Inspiring interns

From August 2018 I spent 3 months at the University of Southampton Science Park, undertaking an internship focusing on its long-term strategic development.
As well as my project, I was also privileged to work alongside the strategic brand consultancy The Escape.
Linked is an article written about my experiences, and circulated to other students looking to undertake internship.

Organised and ran the Southern Unions' Conference

Coordinated and ran a full day conference for 7 different southern Students' Unions. We discussed a variety of topics such as sustainability, the NUS and what it means to be a Sabbatical Officer in the current economic climate.
Although stressful to get off the ground, the day was a massive success and we were able to meet and network with a lot of different CEOs and Sabbs, prompting for further collaboration.

Popped over to our campus in Malaysia

In January 2018 I went with a delegation from the University to visit our Malaysian Campus to assess academic compliance and the student experience. We were able to meet the Campus' new CEO, interview staff and students, and see all the facilities out there. It was incredible to meet the students we had worked with face to face, and I was able to take back a lot of their feedback and incorporate it into Union and University practices.
What's more, I subsequently worked with the then Vice President (International) to create some University strategy pushing forward ways to engage students from the ASEAN area, and how best to incorporate the campus.
